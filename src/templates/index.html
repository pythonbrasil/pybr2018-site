O evento

A PythonBrasil é o maior evento sobre linguagem de programação Python do Brasil. Feito pela comunidade para a comunidade, tem o objetivo de difundir a linguagem, promover a troca de experiências e manter a comunidade crescendo igualmente em público e impacto social. São 6 dias de imersão onde os participantes poderão contribuir para projetos de software livre, participar de treinamentos e adquirir novos conhecimentos com desenvolvedores renomados da comunidade.

Este ano organizada pela comunidade Python de NATAL/RN com o apoio da APyB (Associação Python Brasil).

Keynotes

Aisha Bello

Virtual Systems Engineer at Cisco

In 2015 Aisha won the Malcolm Tredinnick Memorial Django prize for her contribution to the community through her work with DjangoGirls. In 2017 She was also honoured by the Python Software Foundation as a PSF Fellow.

In addition to serving as the vice chair for the Python Users Nigeria Group a.k.a Python Nigeria Community, Aisha organized the first Django Girls event in Nigeria and had helped organize and coach at many Django Girls workshops in Namibia and Nigeria. Currently She servers as a support team member for DjangoGirls. She was one of the founders and co-organizers of PyLadies Nigeria where she teaches women and girls how to code in Python. She also co-organizes and servers as Conference chair for PyCon Nigeria.

Aisha is an ardent Tech and Python community enthusiast with a strong desire and passion for social change, women’s tech education and empowerment in Africa.

Currently Aisha works as an Virtual Systems Engineer for Cisco Systems Nigeria, where she works with enterprise customers as a DataCenter focused Specialist
